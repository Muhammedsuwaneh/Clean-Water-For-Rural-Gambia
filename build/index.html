About Us

The Clean Water For Rural Gambia Organization is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to providing access to clean water for citizens living in cluster of villages in the rural area of The Gambia - West Africa.

Providing Clean Water

Together we can provide clean water for rural Gambians and improve their standard for living
